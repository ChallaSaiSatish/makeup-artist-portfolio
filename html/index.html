About Me

Hello! I'm Manmohan, a passionate and professional makeup artist with over 2 years of experience in creating stunning looks for weddings, parties, photoshoots, and more. My goal is to enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and radiant for any occasion.

I specialize in various makeup techniques, including bridal, party, editorial, and special effects. With a keen eye for detail and a love for creativity, I tailor each look to suit your unique style and preferences.
